Get Involved

Join hundreds of contributors worldwide working together towards the freedom to create.

Blender is being developed by hundreds of contributors from around the world, including studios and individual artists, professionals and hobbyists, scientists and students, VFX experts and animators, and many more. All united by the desire to have access to a fully free and open-source 3D creation pipeline.

The **Blender Foundation** supports and facilitates these goals. Thanks to donations, the **Blender Institute** and **Blender Studio** projects enable a small group of people to work full-time on Blender.

For everything else, we depend on volunteers from the online community to achieve this goal. More help is always welcome!

Development

Volunteers and professionals alike contribute to Blender. This includes developers, technical artists, documenters, translators, designers, and users who test the software and provide feedback.
